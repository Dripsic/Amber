Reservations, Cancellations, No-Shows
  • Reservations must be paid in full after booking, within 1 hour of complete booking.
  • We reserve the right to cancel or amend a reservation in cases of no-shows and suspicions of fraud.
  • Changes or cancellations must be made in writing by email.
  • Cancellations must be made within 24 hours prior to arrival for refunds to be valid.
  • Where cancellation is not received before arrival, the room may be released and a NO-SHOW charge may apply.
